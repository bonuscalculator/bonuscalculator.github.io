About Bonus Calculator

Our Mission

Bonus Calculator exists to simplify the complex process of understanding and calculating bonus payments. We believe that financial clarity should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background or expertise. Our tool empowers employees and employers to make informed decisions about compensation.

What We Do

We provide a modern, brutalist-designed calculator that breaks down bonus calculations into understandable components. Our advanced options allow users to factor in taxes, deductions, overtime, and payment frequency to get accurate, personalized results.

Why Bonus Calculator?

Accurate

Our calculator uses industry-standard formulas and real-world parameters to provide accurate bonus estimates.

Advanced

We offer advanced options for tax calculations, deductions, overtime, and multiple payment frequencies.

Modern Design

Built with brutalist design principles for a clean, professional, and distraction-free experience.

No Signup

Use our calculator instantly - no registration, login, or personal information required.

Free

Open Source

Key Features

  • Base Salary Input: Enter your base salary to start the calculation
  • Bonus Percentage: Calculate bonuses as a percentage of your base salary
  • Tax Calculations: Account for federal, state, and local taxes with customizable rates
  • Deduction Tracking: Include annual deductions in your calculations
  • Payment Frequency: Calculate monthly, quarterly, or annual impacts
  • Overtime Calculations: Apply overtime multipliers (1.5x standard rate)
  • Detailed Breakdown: See line-by-line breakdown of your calculations
  • Real-Time Results: Watch results update as you adjust inputs

Our Values

Transparency: We believe in transparent calculations and honest communication about what our tool can and cannot do.

Privacy: Your data is yours. We don't store, track, or sell personal information. All calculations happen locally on your device.

Accessibility: Financial tools should be accessible to everyone. Our calculator is free and requires no signup or special knowledge.

Accuracy: We continuously improve our calculations and documentation to provide the most accurate results possible.
