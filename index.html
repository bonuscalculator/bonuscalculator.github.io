Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about bonus calculations

How is the bonus calculated?

The bonus is calculated by multiplying your base salary by the bonus percentage. For example, a $50,000 salary with 10% bonus equals $5,000 gross bonus.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit your financial information.

How are taxes calculated?

The tax calculator applies your specified tax rate to the gross bonus amount. You can customize the rate based on your tax bracket.

Can I include overtime?

Yes. Enable the advanced options toggle, check "Include Overtime," and specify your overtime hours for a comprehensive calculation.

What about deductions?

You can specify annual deductions in the advanced options, which will be subtracted from your gross bonus to show your net amount.

Is there a mobile app?

Our calculator is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices. No app download needed‚Äîjust use it in your browser.
